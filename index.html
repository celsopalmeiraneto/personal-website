Bio

Hello! I'm an experienced Systems Analyst who have been working on IT since 2006 and currently working at Adentis.
Over these years I've helped building software in the most diverse areas. To list some: ERPs for Small Businesses, Forest Management Systems used in large factories, and lately, distributed IoT applications.
I've performed various roles in the software process: eliciting requirements; designing software; developing; testing; deploying and supporting it. More than a hundred companies and thousands of users have used the software I helped to ship.
In 2015, together with colleagues, we won the AngelHACK Brooklyn after developing a Google Chrome extension focused in helping developers to find code references on GitHub. We even incorporated a company! Unfortunately, though, it didn't go forth.
On my free time I like to go out with my wife, pet our cats, read, run, watch Formula One and listen to podcasts.
Check my full résumé to know more about me, and, also feel free to reach me out at anytime.
